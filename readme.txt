Enter any city to search in the search box to fetch the information of your desired city or location.
It contains 3 tabs namely 
•	Temperature status
•	Wind speeds
•	Humidity
In temperature status you will find current temperature, maximum and minimum temperature of that place. (Units will be in degree Celsius)

In wind speeds you will come across with wind speed (in km/hr), wind degrees (in degrees) and feels like temperature (in degree Celsius).

In Humidity section you will find humidity of that place given in percentage (%) and sunrise and sunset timings were also provided.
This project is totally coded by me, took help from YouTube. 
This project is based on RAPID API.
